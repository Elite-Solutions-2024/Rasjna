Pasta

"Pasta: the art of combining flour, water, and passion into a delicious masterpiece." "In a world full of choices, pasta will always have a special place on my plate." "Embracing the rich heritage and timeless flavors of Italian pasta." "Each bite of pasta tells a story of tradition, culture, and love."

Deliciousness served on a plate.

Ingredients:

- 250 grams pasta (penne, fusilli, or any other shape) - 2 tablespoons olive oil - 2 cloves garlic, minced - 1 small onion, finely chopped - 1 bell pepper, diced - 1 small zucchini, diced - 1 small carrot, diced - 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved - 1/2 cup canned sweet corn kernels, drained - 1/2 cup canned black olives, sliced - 1 teaspoon dried oregano - 1 teaspoon dried basil - Salt, to taste - Black pepper, to taste - Grated Parmesan cheese, for serving (optional) - Chopped fresh parsley, for garnish (optional)

Instructions:
1. *Cook the Pasta:* - Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. - Add the pasta to the boiling water and cook according to the package instructions until al dente. - Once cooked, drain the pasta in a colander and set aside. Reserve about 1/2 cup of pasta cooking water.
2. *Prepare the Vegetables:* - While the pasta is cooking, heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. - Add minced garlic and chopped onion to the skillet. Sauté for 2-3 minutes until onions are translucent and garlic is fragrant. - Add diced bell pepper, zucchini, and carrot to the skillet. Cook for 5-7 minutes until vegetables are slightly softened but still crisp.
3. *Add Tomatoes, Corn, and Olives:* - Add halved cherry tomatoes, sweet corn kernels, and sliced black olives to the skillet. - Season with dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and black pepper. Stir well to combine.
4. *Combine Pasta and Vegetables:* - Once the vegetables are cooked to your liking, add the cooked pasta to the skillet. - Toss the pasta with the vegetables until everything is well combined. If the mixture seems dry, add some of the reserved pasta cooking water to moisten it.
5. *Serve:* - Once the pasta is heated through and well mixed with the vegetables, remove the skillet from heat. - Serve the veggie pasta hot, garnished with grated Parmesan cheese and chopped fresh parsley if desired.
Enjoy your flavorful and nutritious vegetarian pasta!
