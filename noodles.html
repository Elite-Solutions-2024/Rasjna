Noodles

Noodles. Love. Repeat. Simplicity in a bowl.
All I need is noodles and good company. Less talk, more noodles.

Deliciousness served on a plate.

Ingredients:

- 250 grams chicken breast, thinly sliced - 200 grams egg noodles (or any noodles of your choice) - 1 onion, thinly sliced - 1 bell pepper, thinly sliced - 1 carrot, julienned - 2 cloves garlic, minced - 1-inch piece of ginger, minced - 2 green chilies, sliced (optional) - 2 tablespoons soy sauce - 1 tablespoon oyster sauce - 1 tablespoon chili sauce (adjust to taste) - 1 tablespoon sesame oil - 2 tablespoons vegetable oil - Salt and pepper to taste - Chopped spring onions for garnish (optional) Instructions:

1. Cook the noodles according to the package instructions until they are al dente. Drain and set aside.
2. In a bowl, marinate the chicken slices with soy sauce, sesame oil, minced garlic, minced ginger, salt, and pepper. Let it marinate for about 15-20 minutes.
3. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the marinated chicken and stir-fry until it is cooked through. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.
4. In the same skillet, add a bit more oil if needed. Add the sliced onions, bell pepper, carrot, and green chilies (if using). Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until the vegetables are slightly softened but still crisp.
5. Return the cooked chicken to the skillet with the vegetables.
6. Add the cooked noodles to the skillet along with oyster sauce and chili sauce. Toss everything together until the noodles and chicken are well coated with the sauce.
7. Stir-fry for another 2-3 minutes until everything is heated through and well combined.
8. Taste and adjust seasoning if needed. If you prefer more sauce, you can add a little more soy sauce or chili sauce.
9. Garnish with chopped spring onions if desired.
10. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious chicken noodles! customize the vegetables and adjust the seasoning according to your taste preferences. Enjoy your meal!
